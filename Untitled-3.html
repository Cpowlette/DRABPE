About the Directory

We believe that one of the best ways of increasing the community's "Share of the Wallet" is by giving much-needed exposure to Black businesses that would otherwise struggle for brand recognition and market presence.

The directory includes more than traditional shops, it showcases web and home-based companies working extremely hard to be seen and heard. The collection is a one-stop resource for the greater Durham community to discover and support Black-owned businesses.
