Our Mission

DRABPE is dedicated to fostering economic growth and professional development within the Durham Region's Black community. We provide networking opportunities, mentorship programs, and resources to help entrepreneurs and professionals achieve their business goals.

Through strategic partnerships and community engagement, we're building a stronger, more inclusive business ecosystem that celebrates diversity and drives innovation.

Welcome to DRABPE

Welcome to DRABPE (Durham Region Association of Black Professionals and Entrepreneurs)! We are reintroducing ourselves and our mission: to empower Black professionals, foster entrepreneurship, and strengthen community connections across Durham Region and beyond.

Through mentorship, networking events, youth programs, and business resources, DRABPE creates spaces where Black brilliance thrives. Whether you're an aspiring entrepreneur, established business owner, or professional looking to connect, this is your community.
